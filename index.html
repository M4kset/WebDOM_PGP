Tile clasification

Color

Name

Description

Air

Just air, nothing very special about it.

Spawn

Also known as spawn point, what it does is pretty straight forward, if multiple spawns are set in a level, the game will choose one of them randomly every time the game is started, and then the game coordinates will be normalized to zero starting from the given spawn.

Goal

Once reached, the level is considered complete.

Concrete

The solid platform you have been waiting for.

Ouch / Kill / Red spike / Lava

That gotta hurt, never touch that again.

Liquid / Water

Its density makes you fall more slowly and of course it gives you the capacity of jumping swimming in it.

Climbable Plant

Gives you ability to move freely in both axis, the downsides are that you can't jump from it so easily and it reduces your movement.

Door

Blocks the access to other areas in a level, wanting to pass? get a key. (Don't confuse with dirt, using a key on dirt will give you a dirty key)

Key

It might not look like a key, but with one of these, you can open any door in a level. [These are not saved between levels] (Don't confuse them with flowers, you can't open doors with flowers)

Background / Wall / Dark Concrete

Is the background a little bit sad for you? Don't panic, with these you can finally fill those gaps in your house, even though opening doors and collecting keys create more gaps.

Background Plant

Some greenery for the background, simple as that.

Purple concrete

An alternative to walking on gray concrete and doors, is just painted solid concrete, but has more charm than air.

Pink background / Pink concrete

Intended for those who wanted pink. (this can be used as flowers, despite not being flowers technically)

Grass

If you wanted to step on something more eco-friendly and place plants without getting something oddly looking, this is the choice for you.

Black concrete / Original concrete

In the development process, concrete was the first block to get implemented and was originally black, but was changed to gray since it didn't look good back then, the whole level palette was tweaked to get along the new color, and now, to avoid having black as just a memory from past days, and because I really liked black from the very start but it didn't get along with the old palette and now it does, black is back.
